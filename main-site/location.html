Collins Street

A Life Defining Address

From Wall Street in New York to the Champs-Élysées in Paris, from Orchard Road in Singapore to The Bund in Shanghai, the world’s greatest cities are defined by their most celebrated locations. For Melbourne this is unquestionably Collins Street – one of the most prestigious and coveted addresses in Australia.

Melbourne

One of the World’s Most Liveable Cities

Melbourne offers a diverse and richly rewarding lifestyle, from its shopping and commerce to its cultural delights and magnificent parklands. It’s little wonder Melbourne has been voted one of the world’s most liveable cities on multiple occasions.

NORTHERN CBD PRECINCT

TO THE NORTH OF COLLINS HOUSE

To the north you'll find a variety of major attractions. The State Library of Victoria, with its stunning architecture and expansive collections, is a must-visit.

Nearby, Melbourne Central offers a vibrant shopping experience and features the historic Coops Shot Tower.

Queen Victoria Market, a short distance away, is perfect for experiencing local food, fresh produce, and unique merchandise.

Additionally, the lively laneways such as Hardware Lane and Bourke Street Mall provide excellent dining, shopping, and street performances, encapsulating the dynamic atmosphere of Melbourne's heart.

SOUTHERN CBD PRECINCT

TO THE SOUTH OF COLLINS HOUSE

To the south you'll discover an array of major attractions. The bustling Southbank Promenade offers picturesque views along the Yarra River, lined with cafes, restaurants, and arts venues.

Crown Melbourne, a premier entertainment complex, features a casino, luxury shopping, and diverse dining options.

The Arts Centre Melbourne and the National Gallery of Victoria showcase world-class performances and art collections.

Additionally, the Royal Botanic Gardens provide a tranquil escape with beautifully landscaped gardens and scenic walking paths.

EASTERN CBD PRECINCT

TO THE EAST OF COLLINS HOUSE

To the east you'll encounter several major attractions. The bustling Bourke Street Mall is a premier shopping destination with a mix of flagship stores and boutiques.

The historic Block Arcade and Royal Arcade offer elegant shopping experiences with stunning architecture.

Federation Square, a cultural hub, features iconic venues like the Ian Potter Centre and ACMI.

Nearby, St. Paul's Cathedral stands as a striking Gothic Revival landmark.

These attractions blend Melbourne's rich history, vibrant culture, and modern appeal, making the eastern CBD a must-visit area.

WESTERN CBD PRECINCT

TO THE WEST OF COLLINS HOUSE

To the west you'll find several notable attractions. Southern Cross Station, a major transport hub, features striking modern architecture and offers easy access to various parts of the city.

The Docklands precinct boasts waterfront dining, shopping, and entertainment options, including Marvel Stadium that hosts a variety of sports and entertainment events.

Additionally, the Rialto Towers provide an observation deck with stunning city views, and the Spencer Outlet Centre offers a range of retail opportunities.

These attractions highlight the blend of modern infrastructure and vibrant entertainment in Melbourne's western CBD.
