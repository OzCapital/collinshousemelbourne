Australian Owners Corporation Management

Vibrant Team with a Forward-Thinking Approach

Australian Owners Corporation Management Pty Ltd (AOCM) is the Owners Corporation Manager for Collins House.

AOCM are an experienced and established team with a focus on adding value to the building, not just maintaining it. They ensure that all facets of Owners Corporation compliance are attended to in a timely manner.

They stay current through membership of the industry body, Strata Community Australia, as well as ongoing training. Their specialist legal advisers keep them updated with any changes in legislation that affect the properties they manage.

CLLIX Apartments and Hotels

Collins House Building Management

CLLIX Apartments and Hotels is an Australian Hospitality Provider with over 2,500 apartments and more than 20 properties situated in Brisbane, Melbourne, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and Adelaide, providing seamless experiences all in a CLLIX of a button.

CLLIX manages Collins House on a day-to-day basis under the direction of the Collins House Owners Corporation Committee. CLLIX aims to put the services on the highest level to satisfy the demands of the changing times. The ideology of CLLIX is to combine innovation, top-notch technology, and high-quality customer service.

owners corporation committee

Dedicated Elected Representatives

Collins House has a seven member Owners Corporation Committee who have been elected at the Annual General Meeting to make decisions regarding the direction of the building on behalf of all owners. The Committee Chair liases with CLLIX on a regular basis to ensure the Committee's wishes are conveyed and implemented in the best interest of owners and residents.
