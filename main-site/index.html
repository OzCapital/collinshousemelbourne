Collins Street

A life defining address

From Wall Street in New York to the Champs-Élysées in Paris, from Orchard Road in Singapore to The Bund in Shanghai, the world’s greatest cities are defined by their most celebrated locations. For Melbourne this is unquestionably Collins Street – one of the most prestigious and coveted addresses in Australia.

Collins House

A Building Of Distinction

The Collins House experience begins on the first three floors, where the original grandeur of the heritage-listed Maker’s Mark, built in 1908, façade has been restored. The artful incorporation of stunning original Art Nouveau detailing from the existing 1908 building provides a unique, stately façade and grand entrance hall.

The tower is set back to create an elegant landmark building, both at street level and for the city skyline. Usually reserved for commercial towers, Collins House is the first residential living seen in the premier CBD location of Collins Street for many years.

Spacious, superbly crafted and offering spectacular views, the residences of Collins House provide tailor-made living with a range of multiple storey lofts and expansive single level designs.
